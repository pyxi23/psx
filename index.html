Hourly Currency Calculator

how much currency do you make per hour? (ESTIMATE)

heres how to use this: go to a chest and get your pets on it for a little while. take note of the ticks you get (every time the chest 'ticks' it will give you currency), and put in the lowest and highest ticks you get into the number inputs. *USE DECIMALS!* I cannot stress this enough because the calculator interprets it as a different notation at that point. instead of writing 1,144,000,000, write 1.14 because that's what this calculator uses. if you dont get over 1b, instead of writing 789 or 789,000,000, write 0.789 because that's what this calculator takes. then just press calculate, and you'll see how much currency you will make in the span of an hour!
